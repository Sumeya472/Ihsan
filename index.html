Help Center

Getting Started

Welcome to Ihsan - Islamic Countdown Manager! This app helps you track important events with beautiful countdown timers and Islamic features.

Creating Countdowns

  1. Go to the "Add New Countdown" section
  2. Enter a title for your event
  3. Select the event date using the date picker
  4. Choose a category to organize your countdown
  5. Click "Add Countdown" to save it

Managing Countdowns

  • Set Active: Click any countdown to display it in the main view
  • Edit: Click the pencil icon to modify a countdown
  • Delete: Click the trash icon to remove a countdown
  • Newest First: New countdowns appear at the top of your list

Prayer Times

  • Automatic Updates: Prayer times are automatically calculated for Addis Ababa
  • Current Prayer: The current prayer time is highlighted
  • Countdown: See how much time remains until the next prayer

Advanced Features

  • Themes: Personalize the app with different color schemes
  • Progress Tracking: Visual indicator showing time progress
  • Statistics: Track your countdown activity with detailed stats

Islamic Features

  • Tasbih Counter: Keep track of your dhikr with the digital tasbih counter
  • Qur'an Streak: Build a daily Qur'an reading habit with streak tracking
  • Islamic Calendar: Track Hijri dates and important Islamic events
  • Qibla Finder: Find the direction to the Kaaba from anywhere
  • Daily Dua: Access a collection of authentic Islamic supplications
  • Zakat Calculator: Calculate your Zakat obligation accurately
